Software devLOVEper webseries

The Software DevLOVEper, stylized as The Software DevLOVEper, is an Indian Telugu-language romantic comedy drama web series written and directed by K. Subbu.[1][2] Created by Infinitum Media, the series has an ensemble cast of Shanmukh Jaswanth Kandregula, Vaishnavi Chaitanya, Pruthvi Mukka, Jhakaas Pruthvi and Iduri Sri Priya.

CAST

  • Shanmukh Jaswanth Kandregula as Shanmukh "Shannu" Jaswanth
  • Vaishnavi Chaitanya[4] as Vaishnavi
  • Pruthvi Mukka as DC Don
  • Iduri Sri Priya as Harika
  • Palaparthi Sri Vidya as HR Manager Shruti
  • Jaya Chandra as Manager Aravind
  • Subbu K as Advisor
  • Pravallika Damerla as Priya
